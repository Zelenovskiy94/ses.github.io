SES Hybrid Li-Metal Batteries

The performance of Li-Metal. The manufacturability of Li-ion.

High energy density. Fast-charging. Affordable manufacturing. Introducing the most mature, practical, and independently validated Li-Metal battery for electric vehicles (EVs).

The power of SES Hybrid Li-Metal Batteries.

  • Projected 400 Wh/kg and 1,000 Wh/L, providing longer driving range.

  • Capable of 80% charge in less than 15 minutes

  • Ultra-thin Li-Metal anode reduces battery weight and production costs.

  • Proprietary electrolyte and AI algorithm greatly enhance safety.

  • AI-powered performance optimization.

  • Manufacturable at scale using existing infrastructure and processes.

Watch the video to explore the benefits of the SES Hybrid Li-Metal Battery.

A smarter approach to safety.

Our proprietary AI algorithm is trained on terabytes of data, optimizing battery performance, safety, and charging across a wide variety of applications.

Engineered to scale.

SES has produced more than 15,000 multi-layer Hybrid Li-Metal cells on its own production lines. Our practical approach enables cost savings for OEMs and affordable EVs for everyone..

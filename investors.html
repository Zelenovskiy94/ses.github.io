Charge ahead.

SES’ proprietary Li-Metal anode delivers incredible energy density, enabling EVs with driving ranges that greatly surpass those of today’s vehicles. Our Li-Metal architecture supports fast charging, allowing drivers to recharge 80% of battery capacity in just 15 minutes. SES’ ultra-thin Li-Metal anode also reduces weight and production costs, providing benefits for OEMs and consumers alike.

Furthermore, the SES Hybrid Li-Metal Battery is significantly cheaper to produce than Li-ion over the long term. It can be manufactured at scale in facilities very similar to those used for Li-ion.

SES has already produced more than 15,000 multi-layer Hybrid Li-Metal cells on its own production lines. Our practical approach to manufacturing high-energy-density Li-Metal cells delivers cost savings to automotive OEMs and more affordable mainstream EVs -- with no compromises in performance.

Smarter than all-solid-state

Much of the R&D in the Li-Metal battery space focuses on all-solid-state solutions. However, there are major obstacles to developing production-ready all-solid-state cells: Low energy density, high temperature requirements, expensive materials, and hard-to-scale manufacturing processes.

That’s why the SES Hybrid Li-Metal Battery is a superior solution. It represents a far more practical, better-performing, mature, and complete Li-Metal approach than all-solid-state alternatives.
